Creme Brulee Recipe

The Ingredients and Equipment

  • 2 cups cream
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Saucepan
  • Ramekins
  • Kitchen Torch

Mixing the Ingredients

First, preheat the oven to 325 degrees farenheit. In a saucepan, combine the salt, vanilla bean, and cream. Cook on medium heat until pan is hot. Let pan sit at hot temperature for a few minutes, than discard the vanilla bean. Seperate egg yolks from whites, discarding the whites, and keeping the yolks in a bowl. Next add sugar to the bowl and mix. Now, put the sugar-egg mixture into the cream mixture and stir vigorously.

Cooking and adding sugar topping

Pour created mixture into as many ramekins as necessary, and put ramekins in a baking dish. Fill dish with boiling water which should go halfway up the side of the dish. Bake until the center is set(about 40 minutes, but make sure to monitor carefully) Cool completely at room temp., then refrigerate for several hours. When you are ready to serve the cremebrulee, top each ramekin with one layer og sugar. Finally, use a kitchen torch to brown the sugar ever so slightly. Enjoy!
