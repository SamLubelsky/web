Pour created mixture into as many ramekins as necessary, and put ramekins in a baking dish. Fill dish with boiling water which should go halfway up the side of the dish. Bake until the center is set(about 40 minutes, but make sure to monitor carefully) Cool completely at room temp., then refrigerate for several hours. When you are ready to serve the cremebrulee, top each ramekin with one layer of sugar. Finally, use a kitchen torch to brown the sugar ever so slightly. Enjoy!
